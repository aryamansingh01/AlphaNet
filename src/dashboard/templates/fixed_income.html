Yield Curve Analysis

Curve Metrics

Level

Average yield across the curve. Higher level = tighter monetary conditions.

--

Slope

Difference between long and short rates. Positive = normal curve, negative = inverted.

--

Curvature

Butterfly spread measuring the belly vs wings. Signals butterfly trade opportunities.

--

2s10s

10yr minus 2yr yield. Classic recession signal when negative (inverted).

--

3m10y

10yr minus 3-month yield. Fed's preferred recession predictor when inverted.

--

Inverted

Whether the yield curve is inverted (short rates above long rates).
